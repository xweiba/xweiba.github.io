# Android SDK 及环境配置
按照Android环境安装文档来就行。

# 环境变量
`FLUTTER_SDK_ROOT`:`D:\resource\Flutter\Flutter-SDK`
`Path`:`%FLUTTER_SDK_ROOT%\bin;%FLUTTER_SDK_ROOT%\bin\cache\dart-sdk\bin;%FLUTTER_SDK_ROOT%\.pub-cache\bin`


# 环境检测
`flutter doctor -v` 查看环境详情
